User: Ofainɛ feemɔ nakai nɔŋŋ oha Kwame Nkrumah
Ai: Kwame Nkrumah (19091972) ji Ghana maŋsɔɔlɔ nukpa kɛ maŋ onukpa klɛŋklɛŋ, ni enyiɛ maŋ lɛ hiɛ koni eye ehe kɛjɛ Britain he yɛ 1957. Eji maŋ onukpa ni yɔɔ hewalɛ waa, eto Kpee Gbɔmɛi Akuu lɛ shishi ni ekɛ ehe wo socialist hiɛyaa kɛ shikpɔŋ wulu nɔ ekomefeemɔ mli. Enitsumɔhe nitsumɔi ni hiɛdɔɔ yɔɔ mli kɛ nɔyeli ni yeɔ nɔ lɛ ba naagbee kɛ asraafoi akuu ni jie lɛ yɛ 1966.
